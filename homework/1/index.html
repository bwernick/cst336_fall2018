Programming languages allow us to instruct the computer in a language the computer understands. In turn, the computer translates these instructions into binary, and then follows those instructions.

Just like how there is a large variety of languages that human's use to communicate, there are a large variety of languages humans use to communicate to computers.

This site goes over some general information regarding a few of those languages.
